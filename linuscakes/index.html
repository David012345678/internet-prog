The Birth Of Linus Cakes

Linus Masiga embarked on his journey in the world of confectionery in 2010, setting the stage for a new era of delectable treats in Nairobi, Kenya. With a passion for crafting exceptional cakes, Linus established his bakery with a vision to redefine the art of baking and elevate the expectations of dessert enthusiasts across the region. .

Where it all started

Nestled in the heart of Nairobi, Linus Cakes quickly became a beacon of culinary excellence, captivating the palates of locals and visitors alike. From its humble beginnings, the bakery has blossomed into a beloved establishment renowned for its commitment to quality, innovation, and the art of creating unforgettable cake experiences

Expectations

At Linus Cakes, customers can expect nothing short of perfection. Each creation is a testament to Linus Masiga's unwavering dedication to using the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques. Whether savoring the rich chocolate cake, the velvety red velvet, the classic black forest, or the delicate vanilla cake, patrons can anticipate a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation, setting a new standard for exceptional confectionery delights.
